The goal is to examine the how easily a user can identify political bias in media and creating and news aggregator that quickly and easily helps to give the reader some indication of the political bias of an article, news source, or journalist before clicking. This will focus on news publishers/blogs and not on social media sites. I would like to focus on users that acknowledge the existence of political bias in journalism and would like to avoid social media as a political primary news source
